# Blackburrow

Beneath the rolling hills that divide Qeynos Hills from the vastness of the Karanas lies a wound in the earth known as Blackburrow — a labyrinthine network of tunnels, chambers, and dens carved by the gnolls of **Clan Sabertooth** over generations of bitter patience.

## The Warren

Blackburrow is not merely a cave. It is a city of teeth and claw, its passages worn smooth by a thousand gnoll paws, its air thick with torch smoke and the musk of predators at rest. The deeper one descends, the older the stonework becomes — lower halls that predate Clan Sabertooth entirely, perhaps carved by something that no longer answers to any name.

## Clan Sabertooth

The gnolls who inhabit Blackburrow are disciplined by gnoll standards, organized into hunting parties, ritual circles, and war bands. They raid caravans on the roads above, steal grain from outlying farmsteads, and have killed enough Qeynos guards that the city long ago posted a standing bounty on gnoll pelts.

Their leader, **Darkpaw**, commands from the deepest accessible chamber. He is not merely large — he is deliberate, which makes him dangerous in ways that simple size cannot account for.

## Lore Threads

- The lowest passages of Blackburrow connect to something older. No gnoll speaks of it.
- Clan Sabertooth has trade arrangements with certain goblin factions in Runnyeye — the nature of this arrangement is unknown to the surface world.
- Fippy Darkpaw, the gnoll who endlessly assaults the gates of Qeynos, is a Blackburrow exile — cast out for some transgression that Clan Sabertooth refuses to name.
