# Dark Reflection

In Neriak, the Third Gate, in the quarter known as the Foreign Quarter, the church-militant of the Prince of Hate maintains its offices inside a building that was once a library. The books are still there. They have been rewritten.

**Dark Reflection** is the institutional face of Innoruuk's worship among the dark elves — the organization that trains Shadow Knights, ordains death priests, and adjudicates disputes over who has demonstrated sufficient hatred to merit advancement.

## Doctrine

Innoruuk does not ask for love. He asks for hate — refined, deliberate, structured. Dark Reflection teaches that emotion without discipline is weakness. Rage that breaks things is satisfying but wasteful. The practitioner of true Hate learns to *aim* it, to sustain it, to use it the way a smith uses fire: to forge something that will last.

Shadow Knights trained under Dark Reflection are not berserkers. They are patient.

## Hierarchy

Dark Reflection's hierarchy is based entirely on demonstrated capacity for principled malice. The lowest ranks perform small cruelties. The highest ranks have dedicated years to singular, sustained projects of destruction — entire bloodlines ruined, alliances corroded from within, works of good undone so thoroughly that future generations cannot even name what was lost.
