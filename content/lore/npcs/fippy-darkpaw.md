# Fippy Darkpaw

No one knows exactly when Fippy Darkpaw first appeared outside the western gate of Qeynos. The guards who have served twenty-year careers there grew up hearing stories about him from the guards who trained them. At some point the stories stopped being stories and became simply *fact* — the same category of fact as sunrise or the weight of iron.

Every day, Fippy Darkpaw runs toward the gates of Qeynos.

Every day, he is killed.

Every day, he returns.

## The Exile

The gnolls of Blackburrow do not speak of Fippy Darkpaw. When asked, the rare gnoll in a position to answer will change the subject with an aggression that suggests the topic is not merely uncomfortable but forbidden. What transgression earns exile from Clan Sabertooth — a clan notable for tolerating almost any kind of violence — remains unrecorded.

What is known: he did not choose to leave. He was cast out. And then he ran toward the nearest enemy, which happened to be the city his people had always called a target.

## The Question

Scholars who study the question of whether Fippy Darkpaw is brave, foolish, or simply broken have not reached consensus. The Qeynos city watch stopped asking after the third generation of guardsmen made careers out of the single kill. They simply call him Fippy, which is the closest thing to a name a gnoll has ever received from humans who weren't cursing.

He is, by any reasonable standard, the most persistent creature in Antonica.
